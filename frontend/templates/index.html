What is Perspectivo?


Perspectivo is an online Art-Posting Website.
It allows one to share perspective through the medium of one's own definition of art.

This online community sees & encourages art in forms of poems, music, photos, graphics, videos, in genres accross any bounds.

Create your own blog-profile & show us your artwork.
